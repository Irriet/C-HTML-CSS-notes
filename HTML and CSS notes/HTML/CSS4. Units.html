Units

Absolute units

Those are not affected by anything around them, nor by the device.
  • cm
  • mm
  • inch

Special units

  • px - it's absolute, but relatively scaled. It's the DPI that's used to calculate the resolution. Pixels appear a bit different on high and low resolution devices: they are smaller on high resolution ones.
  • pt - a bit bigger than px
  • pc - a lot pigger than px, it's 12 * pt

Relative units

  • percentage %
  • em - if font size is x, 2em is going to be 2x. It's a proportion value, relative to the standard font size.
  • view width and height - 70vw would be 70*1% of the total viewport (user's visible area of a web page). 400vh is 400% of the viewport.
